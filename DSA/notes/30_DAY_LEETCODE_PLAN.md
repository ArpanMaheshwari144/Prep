## Rules:
1. **30 min khud soch.** Pen paper. Brute force bhi chalega.
2. **Nahi hua → solution dekh. Samajh. Dry run.**
3. **3 din baad wohi question bina dekhe try.**
4. **Skip theek hai — kal kar lena. Chhod mat.**

## Track:

- ✅ Khud solve (bina solution dekhe)
- 🔄 Solution dekh ke samjha (3 din baad retry)
- ❌ Nahi hua (baad mein)
